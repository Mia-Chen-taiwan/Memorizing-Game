# Memorizing Game
Web Memorizing Game with pocker cards.

## Features
- random assigned cards
- counting your score and tried times

### Start playing
Click on two cards a round, and try your best to memorize them. Tried times will +1 once you click on the second card.

If two cards' numbers matched, you can get 10 points and the cards will change color and deacativated.

The game will be completed as all the cards are matched.
